Chicken

A classic meal with chicken could consist of roasted chicken served with seasoned vegetables such as garlic roasted potatoes and carrots. On the side, a mixed green salad with vinaigrette dressing. For dessert, a fruit cobbler or a light lemon sorbet.

Beef

A meal with beef could include a hearty beef stew with tender chunks of beef, carrots, potatoes, and onions simmered in a rich, flavorful broth. Served with crusty bread for dipping, this is a satisfying and comforting meal on a cold day. For dessert, a warm apple crisp with vanilla ice cream could be the perfect finishing touch.

Sushi

A meal with sushi typically consists of various types of rolled or shaped sushi, such as maki rolls, temaki cones, and nigiri. The sushi can be filled with a variety of ingredients, including raw fish, cooked seafood, and vegetables, and is typically accompanied by soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger. Dessert could be a bowl of green tea ice cream, rounding off the meal with a touch of sweetness and a nod to traditional Japanese cuisine.
